Beginner Game Designer

Graduated in Basic Computing, but I have an interrupted baccalaureate of Game Designer.


Focused on Narrative Designer and System Designer, I have a great passion for developing stories, creating worlds and plots. My focus is on medieval fiction and fantasy worlds, political and religious conflicts.


I really like working with mechanics and balancing systems, RPG has always been very present in my life.


I have intermediate knowledge in image editing and drawing and also the basics of graphic design.


I currently operate with a Unity engine, I have basic knowledge of the system, but whenever I think of a new feature I research and learn how to do it.


Brazilian native, but I have intermediate English and eager to learn Japanese.


An enthusiast of the gaming industry, I believe that games are the ultimate medium when it comes to storytelling and getting messages and ideas across to audiences, being a convergence of other standalone media such as movies, music and books.
